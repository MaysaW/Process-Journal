I am currently a Design Computing student at the University of Sydney. After working on multiple projects at university and other freelancing work, I have developed an interest in User Experience (UX), User Interface (UI) and Graphic Design.

I love being able to approach a problem and use creative design thinking to develop concepts and bring a level of physicality to them. Each of my main three design areas fascinates me as I enjoy finding the right balance between meeting a users wants and needs with the restrictions of a design brief, to develop innovative solutions.

In my near future, I want to harness the skills I have developed to make a difference in peoples' lives and make even the smallest of interactions, those interactions you would not even have a second thought about, enjoyable.

I whole heartedly believe that design is not just thinking up new concepts or ways of doing things - it's making the current systems and products in place, reach their maximum potential allow users the best experience possible.
